Light

The Light class is used to represent lights in the Scene. Lights can be used for things like the sun or lava.

Properties:

  • home

    A link to the Scene that created the Light.

  • x

    The x coordinate of the center of the Light.

  • y

    The y coordinate of the center of the Light.

  • intensity

    The inner radius of the Light.

  • radius

    The outer radius of the Light.


Methods:

  • constructor(String name, String color, float x, float y, float innerRadius, float outerRadius, Scene home)

    Sets the corresponding properties to the parameters.

  • centerAt(Vertex p)

    Moves the light to p.
