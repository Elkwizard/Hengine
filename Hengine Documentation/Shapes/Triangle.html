Triangle

The Triangle class is used for storing values about triangles. It can be drawn by passing it as a parameter into Artist.draw().triangle.

Properties:

  • vertices

    An Array of Vertex objects containing the vertices of the Triangle.


Methods:

  • constructor(Vertex v1, Vertex v2, Vertex v3)

    Creates a triangle with vertices v1, v2, and v3.

  • middle()

    Returns the coordinates of the center of the Triangle as a Vertex.
