Circle

The Circle class stores a x, y, and radius of a circle. It can be drawn by passing it as a parameter into Artist.draw().circle.

Properties:

  • x

    The x coordinate of the center of the circle.

  • y

    The y coordinate of the center of the circle.

  • radius

    The radius of the circle.


Methods:

  • constructor(float x, float y, float radius)

    Creates a Circle with position (x, y) and a radius of radius.
